TV Shows

  • Mr. Robot - A drama centered around Elliot Alderson, played by Rami Malek, who is a Cyber Security Engineering by day and a vigilante hacker by night. The show portrays a realistic take on the world of hacking, and is a must see for anyone in the Cyber Security industry. The show also has great cinematography, impeccable writing, and phenomonal acting by Rami Malek and others.
  • It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia - One of the greatest comedy shows of all time. Each character is so well written, the episodes practically write themselves. If you are a fan of Seinfeld you will definitely love this.
  • The Boys - This shows brings a nice change of pace to the monotony of Super Hero movies and TV shows coming out. If you are a fan of Watchmen, this will definitely be something up your alley.
  • Silicon Valley

Movies

  • Ex Machina
  • Akira - An anime movie known for its groundbreaking animation. The movie take place in the dystopian future of Neo-Tokyo, a city built from the ashes of a nuclear-bombed Tokyo. This is a great movie for those of who are fans of cyber-punk aesthetics such as Blade Runner.

Podcasts

  • The Joe Rogan Experience
  • Tigerbelly
  • Rooster Teeth Podcast - This podcast is made by the company Rooster Teeth, a company based in Austin known for its show Red vs. Blue, among others. They discuss the current events of movies, gaming, tech, aviation, and Austin/Texas. If you live in Austin or are a fan of any of those topics, I would highly recommending listening to this podcast.
